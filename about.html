About Us

Nathan Wainwright - Pastor

In August 2015, Nathan and his family moved from New Hampshire to come lead Community Christian Church. Nathan grew up in Illinois, and received a Masters in Church Leadership at Lincoln Christian Seminary.

Mollie Macias - Youth and Children's Director

Mollie grew up in the church and has served as the Children's Director since 2003. She was raised in nearby Springdale. She and her husband, Jorge, have two children ages 17 and 5.
