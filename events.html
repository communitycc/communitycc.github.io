Upcoming Events

Month of October - Food Drive

During October, we will be gathering items to donate to Cooperative Emergency Outreach. Please bring your donations to church and place them in the large red tub outside of Pastor Nathan's office. Items of greatest need are below, but feel free to donate anything else.

  • Canned meat
  • Cereal
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Baby food
  • Tooth brushes
  • Baby formula

November 1 - Time Changes

Not only will this be the end of Daylight Saving Time, but we will also be swapping our Worship and Sunday School hours. Sunday School will begin at 9:30am, and the Worship Service will be moved to 10:30.

November 17 - Christ on Campus Thanksgiving

This is an annual mission of putting on a Thanksgiving Banquet for students at the Rockhouse. See Karen Hendrix if you are interested in helping. We will need people to bake desserts, serve the food, and cleanup afterwards.
