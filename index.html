About Us

It's time to be honest, no one has it "all together".

What if I told you there was a place that you could seek truth and seek God without being judged for the decisions you've made?

We've all made mistakes and we beat ourselves up for them enough on our own, we don't need a religious "pile on".

Ain't nobody got time for that!

We want to invite you to a different kind of church, not a perfect church. At Community Christian Church we believe church can be exciting, meaningful, and life-changing. Come casual and enjoy the relaxed, friendly atmosphere, where people accept you for who you are and want to join you in your journey to find God.

We invite you to join us and see how God can change your life!

Nathan Wainwright

Pastor

In August 2015, Nathan and his family moved from New Hampshire to come lead Community Christian Church. Nathan grew up in Illinois and received a Masters in Church Leadership at Lincoln Christian Seminary. His wife Chrissy helps in various roles at the church. They live in Fayetteville with their young son, Eli.

Mollie Macias

Children's Director

Mollie grew up in the church and has served as the Children's Director since 2003. She was raised in nearby Springdale. She and her husband, Jorge, have two children ages 18 and 7.

Toni Robertson

Youth Director

Toni is the Youth Director here at CCC. She was raised in Pine Bluff, Arkansas until the age of 18, then moved to Siloam Springs to pursue a degree at John Brown University in Intercultural Studies and Family and Human Services. She will graduate with her Bachelor's degree in 2018, then move on to attend graduate school where she will obtain a Master's in Counseling.
